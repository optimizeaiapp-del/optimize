Features

Everything you need to improve your grades and optimize your assignments

Teacher-Specific Feedback

Get personalized suggestions tailored to your teacher's marking style and rubric requirements. Our AI analyzes your teacher's preferences and provides feedback that matches their expectations.

  • Select from preset teacher profiles or create a custom profile
  • AI adapts to your teacher's marking style and preferences
  • Receive feedback aligned with your specific rubric
  • Understand what your teacher values most in assignments

Structured Analysis

Receive clear breakdowns with grade estimates and actionable feedback organized by category. Understand exactly where you can improve.

  • Get estimated grades based on your teacher's marking style
  • Detailed feedback organized by category (structure, clarity, evidence, etc.)
  • Clear explanations of why you received specific feedback
  • Actionable suggestions for improvement

Smart Rewrites

Generate polished versions that maintain your voice while improving clarity and structure. Choose your preferred format and rewrite strength.

  • Maintain your unique writing voice and style
  • Adjustable rewrite strength (Light, Balanced, or Max)
  • Multiple output formats (Essay, Paragraph, Bullet Points, or Custom)
  • Improve clarity, structure, and evidence without losing your ideas

Image Analysis

Upload photos of rubrics, artwork, or projects. Our AI analyzes images visually, not just text, to provide comprehensive feedback.

  • Upload images of assignment instructions and rubrics
  • Analyze artwork and visual projects
  • Visual understanding of your work, not just OCR
  • Support for both text and image inputs

Feedback Refinement

Not satisfied with the analysis or rewrite? Provide feedback and get refined results that better match your needs.

  • Refine analysis based on your specific concerns
  • Iterate on rewrites until you're satisfied
  • Continuous improvement based on your feedback
  • Full control over the optimization process
