Getting Started

New to OptimizeAI? Follow these simple steps to start optimizing your assignments

1. Check Your Institution's Policies

Before using OptimizeAI, make sure you understand your school's academic integrity policies regarding AI assistance. OptimizeAI is designed as a learning tool to help you improve, but it's important to use it in accordance with your institution's guidelines.

2. Prepare Your Assignment

Gather your assignment instructions (or rubric if you have one) and your draft. You can provide these as text or upload images. For visual projects, photos work great!

3. Select or Create a Teacher Profile

Choose a teacher profile that matches your teacher's marking style from our preset options, or select "Other" to create a custom profile. Add your teacher's name and describe their marking preferences to get the most accurate feedback.

4. Enter Your Assignment Details

Paste your assignment instructions or upload a photo of the rubric in the "Assignment Instructions" section. Then paste your draft or upload a photo of your work in the "Your Draft" section.

5. Get Your Analysis

Click "Analyze Assignment" and wait a few seconds. You'll receive detailed feedback organized by category, along with an estimated grade based on your teacher's marking style.

6. Generate a Rewrite (Optional)

If you want to improve your draft, use the "Generate Rewrite" feature. Choose your preferred format (Essay, Paragraph, Bullet Points, or Custom) and adjust the rewrite strength slider to your preference.

7. Refine as Needed

Not satisfied with the results? Use the feedback section to tell the AI what you'd like to change, and it will refine the analysis or rewrite based on your input.
