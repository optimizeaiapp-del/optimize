About OptimizeAI

Helping students improve their grades through personalized AI feedback

Our Mission

OptimizeAI was created to help students understand their teachers' expectations and improve their assignments accordingly. We believe that every student deserves personalized feedback that matches their teacher's marking style, helping them learn more effectively and achieve better grades.

Our platform uses advanced AI technology to analyze assignments, provide detailed feedback, and generate improvements—all tailored to how your specific teacher grades work.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike generic writing tools, OptimizeAI focuses on teacher-specific feedback. We understand that different teachers value different things—some prioritize structure, others value creativity, and some emphasize evidence and citations.

By allowing you to select or create a teacher profile, we ensure that the feedback you receive is relevant to your actual grading criteria. This personalized approach helps you improve in ways that matter to your teacher.

Our Commitment to Academic Integrity

OptimizeAI is designed as a learning tool to help students understand their work better and improve their writing skills. We encourage students to use our platform to learn and grow, not to bypass the learning process.

We strongly recommend that all users check their institution's academic integrity policies before using our service. Our goal is to support your learning journey, not to replace it.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. Your assignments and data are processed securely and are not stored or shared. We use your information only to provide the analysis and feedback you request, and we never sell or share your data with third parties.

For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
