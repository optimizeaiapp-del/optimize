1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use OptimizeAI, you may provide:

  • Assignment instructions and drafts (text or images)
  • Teacher profile information
  • Feedback and refinement requests

Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information, such as:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Process your assignments and generate analysis
  • Provide feedback and rewrites based on your requests
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

We do not use your information for marketing purposes or share it with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Important: Your assignments and personal content are not stored on our servers. Data is processed in real-time to generate your analysis and is then discarded.

We may retain minimal technical information (such as usage statistics) for service improvement purposes, but this does not include your assignment content or personal information.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Secure data transmission using encryption
  • Processing through secure AI services
  • No permanent storage of assignment content
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

OptimizeAI uses third-party AI services (such as OpenAI) to process your assignments. These services have their own privacy policies. We ensure that any third-party services we use maintain appropriate security standards.

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what information we collect about you
  • Request information about how your data is used
  • Request that we not process your information (by not using the service)
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Since we do not store your assignment content, there is no stored data to access or delete.

7. Children's Privacy

OptimizeAI is intended for use by students, which may include minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
