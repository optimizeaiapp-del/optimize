How It Works

Optimize your assignments in just a few simple steps

1

Select Your Teacher Profile

Choose a teacher profile that matches your teacher's marking style, or create a custom profile. You can add additional notes about your teacher's preferences to help the AI provide more accurate feedback.

If your teacher isn't in our preset list, select "Other" and describe their marking style. You can also add their name so the analysis will say "[Teacher Name] is grading your work."

2

Provide Assignment Details

Enter your assignment instructions or upload a photo of the rubric. You can also upload images of your artwork or project if it's a visual assignment. The AI will analyze both text and images to understand the full context.

For text-based assignments, simply paste your instructions. For visual projects, upload a clear photo of your work.

3

Submit Your Draft

Paste your assignment draft or upload a photo of your work. The AI will analyze your submission based on your teacher's marking style and the assignment requirements.

You can provide either text or images—or both. The AI uses advanced vision capabilities to understand visual content, not just extract text.

4

Get Analysis & Grade Estimate

Receive detailed feedback organized by category, along with an estimated grade based on your teacher's marking style. The analysis will explain what you did well and where you can improve.

Click "Explain Results" to see a detailed breakdown of your grade and feedback.

5

Generate Rewrite (Optional)

If you want to improve your draft, use the "Generate Rewrite" feature. Choose your preferred output format (Essay, Paragraph, Bullet Points, or Custom) and adjust the rewrite strength:

  • Light: Gentle polish while maintaining your original voice
  • Balanced: Clear rewrite with improvements to structure and clarity
  • Max: Full makeover with significant improvements
6

Refine with Feedback

Not satisfied with the results? Provide feedback on what you'd like to change, and the AI will refine the analysis or rewrite based on your specific concerns.

You can iterate multiple times until you're happy with the output.
