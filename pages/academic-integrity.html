What is Academic Integrity?

Academic integrity means being honest and ethical in your academic work. It involves:

  • Submitting work that is your own (or properly cited)
  • Following your institution's rules about collaboration and assistance
  • Being transparent about the help you receive
  • Learning and understanding the material, not just getting good grades

OptimizeAI as a Learning Tool

OptimizeAI is designed to help you learn and improve your writing skills. It's not meant to replace your own work or thinking. Here's how to use it responsibly:

  • Use feedback to learn: Read the analysis carefully and understand why certain changes are suggested
  • Maintain your voice: Use rewrites as inspiration, but make sure the final work reflects your understanding and style
  • Understand the content: Don't submit work you don't understand or can't explain
  • Follow your school's rules: Some schools allow AI assistance with disclosure, others prohibit it entirely

What Different Schools May Allow

Academic policies vary widely:

  • Some schools allow AI assistance if you disclose it
  • Some schools allow AI for brainstorming but not for final drafts
  • Some schools prohibit all AI assistance
  • Some schools have specific rules about what constitutes acceptable use

It is your responsibility to know and follow your institution's specific policies.

Best Practices for Using OptimizeAI

  • Check policies first: Always review your institution's academic integrity policies before using any AI tool
  • Use for learning: Focus on understanding the feedback and improving your skills, not just getting a better grade
  • Be transparent: If your school requires disclosure, be honest about using AI assistance
  • Don't submit directly: Review and revise any AI-generated content to ensure it reflects your understanding
  • Maintain academic honesty: Use OptimizeAI to help you learn, not to bypass the learning process

What OptimizeAI Cannot Do

OptimizeAI cannot:

  • Guarantee that your use complies with your institution's policies
  • Prevent academic integrity violations
  • Replace your own learning and understanding
  • Ensure you won't face consequences for misuse

You are solely responsible for how you use OptimizeAI and any consequences that result.

If You're Unsure

If you're not sure whether using OptimizeAI is allowed at your institution:

  • Check your course syllabus and academic integrity policies
  • Ask your teacher or professor directly
  • Contact your institution's academic integrity office
  • When in doubt, err on the side of caution
